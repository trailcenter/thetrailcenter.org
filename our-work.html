Our Work

The TRAIL Center uses a four-branch structure to move trauma knowledge into research, support, education, and institutional change.

Organizational Structure

One Center. Four connected pathways.

The TRAIL Center is organized around four branches: TRAIL Lab, TRAIL Speaks, TRAIL Academy, and TRAIL Forward. Each branch has a distinct purpose, but all four work together to advance trauma-responsive systems.

This structure allows the Center to connect research with lived experience, training with practice, and systems-change work with real-world policy, procedure, program, and protocol development.
