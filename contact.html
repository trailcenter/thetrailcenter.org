Important Note

The TRAIL Center provides research, education, support resources, training, and systems-change work. The Center does not provide emergency services, crisis intervention, legal representation, or clinical therapy.

If you are in immediate danger or experiencing an emergency, call 911. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, contact a local crisis line or emergency service in your area.
