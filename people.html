TRAIL Center Leadership and Team

People

A growing team committed to advancing trauma-responsive systems through research, support, training, education, and policy change.

Dr. Lori L. Hall

Founder and Director, TRAIL Center
Director, TRAIL Lab

Dr. Lori L. Hall is the founder and director of the TRAIL Center. Her work focuses on trauma, crime, victimization, maladaptive coping, justice systems, and trauma-responsive change.

Dr. Hall directs the Center’s overall vision, research agenda, training development, partnerships, and systems-change work. Her work bridges criminology, trauma research, lived experience, teaching, advocacy, and institutional reform.
