Support the Mission

Your support helps advance trauma-focused research, educational resources, professional training, support initiatives, and systems-change work.

Contact About SupportVisit Store

The TRAIL Center is building trauma-responsive pathways through research, support, training, education, and policy change.

Support helps the Center transform trauma knowledge into action through the development of research, training, resources, and trauma-responsive programming.

What support helps fund

  • Trauma-focused research projects
  • Research Assistant opportunities
  • Community education materials
  • Workshops and resource development
  • Practitioner training tools
  • TRAIL Academy curriculum development
  • Support resources for trauma-affected individuals and communities
  • Policy briefs and systems-change tools
  • Program evaluation and research translation
  • Website, technology, and administrative infrastructure

Ways to support

Make a Contribution

Contributions help sustain research, training, resources, and trauma-responsive programming. Donation button coming soon.

Sponsor a Project

Organizations, agencies, businesses, and individuals may support specific TRAIL Center projects, workshops, research initiatives, or public education efforts.

Support Training Access

Support may help make trauma-responsive training more accessible to practitioners, advocates, community organizations, and groups with limited resources.

Support TRAIL Lab Research

Support trauma-focused research, Research Assistant development, conference presentations, and research dissemination.

Support TRAIL Speaks Resources

Support workshops, mentorship resources, support guides, and educational materials for people affected by trauma.

Support TRAIL Forward Systems Change

Support policy briefs, model protocols, organizational tools, and research-to-practice resources.

Important Note

The TRAIL Center is an independent center. Contributions may not be tax-deductible unless and until the Center obtains nonprofit tax-exempt status or operates through a qualified fiscal sponsor.

Please consult your tax advisor regarding any contribution.
