There are multiple ways to contribute to trauma-responsive research, support, training, education, and systems change.

Research Assistant Opportunities

TRAIL Lab offers opportunities for individuals interested in trauma-focused research. Research Assistants may contribute to projects related to trauma, coping, victimization, offending, justice involvement, practitioner experiences, systems response, and life-course outcomes.

  • Literature reviews
  • Article summaries
  • Survey and interview development
  • Data collection
  • Qualitative coding
  • Quantitative analysis
  • Program evaluation
  • Conference posters and research presentations
  • Manuscript preparation

To apply: Email a resume, unofficial transcript if applicable, and a brief interest statement to lab@thetrailcenter.org.

TRAIL Academy Fellow Opportunities

TRAIL Academy offers opportunities for individuals interested in training, education, curriculum development, and professional learning.

  • Training development
  • Workshop planning
  • Curriculum support
  • Educational resource development
  • Practitioner toolkits
  • Slide deck development
  • Training handouts
  • Certificate-based learning materials

To apply: Email a resume, unofficial transcript if applicable, and a brief interest statement to academy@thetrailcenter.org.
