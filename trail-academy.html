TRAIL Academy

Training and education for professionals who work with trauma-affected populations.

Request TrainingBecome a TRAIL Academy Fellow

TRAIL Academy develops and delivers trauma-responsive trainings, workshops, educational resources, and professional learning opportunities for criminal justice practitioners, advocates, behavioral health professionals, and others who work with trauma-affected populations.

TRAIL Academy is designed to help professionals understand trauma, recognize its impact, respond more effectively, and reduce harm in practice.

Purpose

The purpose of TRAIL Academy is to translate trauma knowledge into practical training and professional education. TRAIL Academy provides training that helps professionals move beyond basic trauma awareness toward trauma-responsive practice.

Who TRAIL Academy trains

  • Law enforcement professionals
  • Corrections professionals
  • Probation and parole officers
  • Victim advocates
  • Domestic violence advocates
  • Sexual assault advocates
  • Behavioral health professionals
  • Social service providers
  • Case managers
  • Court and legal professionals
  • Community-based practitioners
  • Educators and youth-serving professionals

Training focus areas

Trauma-Responsive Practice

How trauma affects behavior, communication, memory, emotional regulation, trust, coping, and help-seeking.

Trauma and the Criminal Justice System

The role of trauma in victimization, offending, justice involvement, system contact, and institutional response.

Trauma and Victim Response

Trauma-responsive communication, victim engagement, disclosure, support, safety, and systems navigation.

Trauma and Offending

How trauma can shape coping, survival behavior, aggression, substance use, avoidance, and justice involvement.

Practitioner Trauma

Secondary trauma, vicarious trauma, burnout, moral injury, compassion fatigue, and organizational stress.

Trauma and Behavioral Health

The connection between trauma, mental health, substance use, maladaptive coping, and systems involvement.

Training Formats

TRAIL Academy can develop and deliver training in multiple formats, from one-time workshops to customized training series.

  • In-person trainings
  • Virtual trainings
  • Professional development sessions
  • Conference presentations
  • Custom agency trainings
  • Multi-session training series
  • Certificate-based learning opportunities
  • Training guides and toolkits
  • Online learning modules
